# Scoring Rubric (0-10)

Score each dimension 0-2, then sum.

1) Problem clarity
- Is the problem concrete and real?

2) Outcome clarity
- Are success metrics/time horizons explicit?

3) Feasibility
- Can this be executed with available time/skill/resources?

4) Risk posture
- Are major risks identified with mitigations/rollback?

5) Leverage/impact
- If this works, does it materially move the needle?

## Result bands
- 0-3: weak (reframe before action)
- 4-6: workable with major gaps
- 7-8: strong plan with manageable risk
- 9-10: exceptional clarity + high leverage

Always include:
- confidence: low/medium/high
- what would increase score fastest
- what could drop score quickly
