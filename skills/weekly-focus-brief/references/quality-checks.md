# Quality Checks

Before finalizing the brief:

- [ ] Momentum summary is 2–4 lines and not vague.
- [ ] Patterns are capped at 5 bullets.
- [ ] Themes are distinct and non-overlapping.
- [ ] Risk and leverage sections each include a first move.
- [ ] Focus actions are concrete, not generic.
- [ ] Total focus actions are between 5 and 10.
- [ ] Focus actions are ranked highest-impact first.
- [ ] Tone is direct, practical, and slightly opinionated.
